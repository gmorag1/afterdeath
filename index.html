Did this just happen? Do these three things first.

1️⃣

Get legal pronouncement

If unexpected or at home, call 911. Under hospice or hospital care, the attending doctor handles this. Do not move the body until authorized.

Call 911
2️⃣

Contact a funeral home

They will arrange transport. You don't need to make all decisions now — just get someone on the phone. They are available 24/7 and will guide you.

Find a Funeral Home
3️⃣

Call someone to be with you

You should not be alone right now. Call family, a close friend, or neighbor. If you're in emotional crisis, text or call 988 — someone will answer.

Call/Text 988

Everything else can wait. These are the only three things that matter right now.
